# Make Rhythm Visible — Product Standard

This app should feel Apple-quality: elegant, intuitive, minimal, easy to visualize, almost magical, and usable by all ages.

The core promise is:

Play it. See it. Understand it.

The app should help users record a short snare, practice pad, tabletop, or clapped rhythm, detect the hits, visualize the rhythm, and eventually render simple percussion notation.

This should not feel like a rough developer demo, dashboard, documentation tool, or generic music utility. It should feel premium, calm, visual, and memorable.
